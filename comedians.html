Glasgow Comedians

Kevin Bridges

Kevin Andrew Bridges (born 13 November 1986) is a Scottish stand-up comedian. Known for his observational comedy and sharp wit, he has become one of Scotland's most famous comedic exports. His 2012 television series Kevin Bridges: What's the Story? was based on his stand-up routines and further solidified his place in British comedy.

Fern Brady

Fern Marie Brady is a Scottish comedian, podcaster, and writer. She gained recognition for her stand-up comedy at prestigious competitions like the Edinburgh Fringe Festival. Fern has appeared on panel shows such as *8 Out of 10 Cats* and co-created the popular podcast *Wheel of Misfortune* in 2020.

Frankie Boyle

Francis Martin Patrick Boyle is a Scottish comedian and writer. Boyle first gained widespread recognition as a regular panellist on the comedy show *Mock the Week* from 2005 until 2009. Known for his dark humor and sharp wit, Frankie has become one of the most controversial and outspoken voices in British comedy.

Des Clarke

Desmond Martin Clarke is a Scottish stand-up comedian and television/radio presenter. He currently presents *Heart Breakfast* on Heart Scotland, writes a weekly column for the *Daily Record*, and hosts the BBC Radio Scotland topical quiz show *Breaking the News*.
