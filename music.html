Glasgow Music Venues

Barrowlands

Known for its iconic neon lights and lively atmosphere, Barrowlands is a legendary venue in Glasgow’s music scene. It hosts live performances from both established and up-and-coming artists across various genres.

King Tut's Wah Wah Hut

King Tut’s is a small but mighty venue in the heart of Glasgow. It's known for its intimate setting and rich history of launching the careers of iconic bands, such as Oasis.

OVO Hydro

The OVO Hydro is Glasgow’s premier arena for large-scale concerts and events. Hosting major international artists, it offers an unforgettable concert experience with state-of-the-art facilities.

Cathouse

A beloved venue for rock and metal fans, Cathouse is Glasgow’s longest-running rock club. Its dark, gritty atmosphere and high-energy events make it a favorite for fans of alternative music.

Glasgow Concert Halls

The Glasgow Concert Halls are home to a wide range of musical performances, from classical orchestras to contemporary music. It’s a cultural landmark in the city’s music scene.
